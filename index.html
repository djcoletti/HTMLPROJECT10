About Us

Durham House of Pets

History

Durham House of Pets was founded by Alexzander Simmons on October 10th, 1982 in Miami, Florida. DHP is a retail organization producing an infinite number of pet products and services over a wide variety of animals. We offer dog, cat, bird, reptile, and fish products along with hamsters and guinea pigs. When Durham House of Pets was first established the products sold included supplies and apparel for dogs, cats, birds, reptiles, and fish. Over the years DHP has grown immensely with the production of charities, nursery/adoption centers and the expansion of store locations throughout the United States.

Why Use Us

DHP is a unique corporation and we differ from other pet stores due to our customer/employee experience and satisfaction as well as how we operate our business. The interaction between our employees and customers creates a comfortable and relaxed environment while providing answers or help always to any questions that are asked. DHP products are durable and high quality no matter the situation for each animal. Our stores are in high populated areas which attract many customers. Also, our stores are easy to get to while being in a suitable environment with appropriate access for citizens to feel adequate while they shop.
